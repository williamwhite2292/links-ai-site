# Links AI Homepage Copy

This is the first-pass homepage structure and messaging for the `Links AI` website.

The goal is not to sound futuristic.
The goal is to sound clear, capable, and credible.

## 1. Hero

### Headline options

Option A:

**Practical AI systems for service businesses**

Option B:

**AI implementation for real business workflows**

Option C:

**We help service businesses put AI to work inside their operations**

Option D:

**AI support for the workflows your business already runs**

### Recommended supporting copy

From focused workflow improvements to larger AI systems over time, `Links AI` helps service businesses design, implement, and support practical AI solutions inside the tools, software, and processes they already use.

### Primary CTA

- `Book an exploratory call`

### Secondary CTA

- `See how we work`

## 2. Credibility Strip

Short line under hero:

We focus on practical implementation:

- workflow automation
- AI add-ons to existing software
- lead follow-up, sales funnel, and outreach support
- document and data classification
- SEO, content, and reporting support

## 3. What We Help With

### Section headline

**Common first use cases**

### Intro copy

Most businesses do not need a giant AI rollout on day one.
They need one practical improvement that saves time, reduces manual work, or helps revenue move faster.

### Example cards

#### Operations

Reduce manual admin work with AI systems for intake, routing, follow-up, reporting, and recurring internal workflows.

#### Sales and follow-up

Improve response speed, follow-up consistency, and sales funnel movement with AI-supported outreach, lead-handling, and pipeline support workflows.

#### Content and visibility

Support SEO, content creation, and ongoing publishing workflows with structured AI assistance that fits your real process.

#### Classification and organization

Use AI to classify receipts, documents, customer requests, and operational data inside the systems you already use.

#### Existing software add-ons

Add focused AI capabilities to the custom software or internal tools your business already depends on, without forcing a full platform change.

## 4. How We Work

### Section headline

**A practical path from idea to working system**

### Step 1

**Free exploratory call**

We start with a real conversation about the business, the workflow, and where AI could create leverage now.

### Step 2

**Opportunity map**

We identify the most useful place to start, define scope, and recommend the first implementation path.

We design and implement the agreed workflow or system inside the client’s real environment.

### Step 4

**Ongoing improvement**

We stay involved after launch to refine, support, and expand the system over time.

## 8. Tone Rules

The homepage should:

- sound practical
- sound calm and capable
- stay broad enough for multiple service businesses
- use concrete workflow examples
- hint at larger systems without leading with them
- make room for both operations and growth-related use cases

The homepage should avoid:

- “autonomous company” language as the main pitch
- heavy agent jargon
- overpromising transformation
- sounding like a generic automation agency
